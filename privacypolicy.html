Salon Booking System Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our Salon Booking System.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly when using our Salon Booking System, including:

  • Your name
  • Contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Appointment preferences
  • Payment information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Facilitate and process salon bookings
  • Communicate with you about appointments and updates
  • Improve our Salon Booking System
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our services

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the Salon Booking System services. We may share information with:

  • Salon staff to fulfill your booking
  • Payment processors for transaction purposes
  • Legal authorities if required by law

Your Choices

You can choose not to provide certain information, but it may affect your ability to use our Salon Booking System.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access and use.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us .
